# Category Page Requirements

## User Stories

### Navigation & Header
- As a user, I want to see breadcrumbs to understand my location within the category structure.
- As a user, I want to see the Category Title (e.g., "Electronics") clearly displayed.

### Filtering & Sorting
- As a user, I want to see a sidebar (on desktop) or a toggle button (on mobile) to access filters.
- As a user, I want to filter products by sub-categories (e.g., TV & Video, Computers).
- As a user, I want to filter by price ranges or enter a custom min/max price.
- As a user, I want to search for and select specific brands to filter by.
- As a user, I want to filter by customer rating (e.g., 4 stars & up).
- As a user, I want to filter by special offers (e.g., Rollback, Clearance).
- As a user, I want to sort the results by Best Match, Price, Rating, or Newest.

### Product Listing
- As a user, I want to see the total count of results found.
- As a user, I want to view products in a grid layout.
- As a user, I want each product card to show the image, price (with discounts), title, rating, and shipping info.
- As a user, I want to see badges like "Best Seller" or "Rollback" on relevant items.
- As a user, I want to be able to add an item to the cart directly from the list.

### Pagination
- As a user, I want to see pagination controls to navigate through multiple pages of results.
