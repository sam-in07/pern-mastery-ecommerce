# Shopping Cart Page Requirements

## User Stories

### Cart Overview
- As a user, I want to see a clear title indicating I am in the cart and how many items are included.

### Item Management
- As a user, I want to see each item's image and title to confirm what I am buying.
- As a user, I want to see the price per item and any discounts (strikethrough price).
- As a user, I want to see stock status (e.g., "In stock") and badges (e.g., "Rollback").
- As a user, I want to be able to change the quantity of an item.
- As a user, I want to be able to remove an item from the cart.
- As a user, I want to be able to "Save for later" if I'm not ready to buy now.

### Order Summary
- As a user, I want to see a summary breakdown including Subtotal, Shipping cost, and Estimated taxes.
- As a user, I want to see the final Total amount clearly displayed.
- As a user, I want to click "Continue to Checkout" to proceed with my purchase.
- As a user, I want to see a prompt to sign in for a better experience if I am not logged in.
